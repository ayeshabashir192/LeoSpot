We provide our clients with dedicated accounts supporting SEPA, Faster Payments, Bacs Direct Credit and CHAPS

LeoSpot is one of a small number of non-bank payment service providers to have direct access to the UK’s Bacs and Faster Payments services, allowing us to hold an account at the Bank of England (BoE). This allows us to settle payment obligations in central bank money, instead of going through an intermediary bank.

Large number of processed payments

In under 6 years, our payment partners processed more than £145 billion worth of payments on behalf of their clients. Our direct partners have worked with Sage to develop payment solutions already used by many businesses and accountants across the UK. Every payment service is available to our customers through our API which can be easily integrated into existing applications and systems.
